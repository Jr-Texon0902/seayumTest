About Us

SeaYum is a restaurant that was recently established this year 2021, already starting to create wonderful times and memories in the Philippines. It is still hiring crews and members that can help to pursue its goals.

Mission

To provide everyone with delight and safe food by serving great-tasting foods.

Vision

To become the best food restaurant in the Philippines, specializing in sea foods.

Values

Truth, respect, and faith in our dealings with our customers, crews, and members. Integrating integrity, honesty, and business ethics into every aspect of our restaurant's operations. Creativity, innovation, collaboration, and integration are all important aspects of our development.
